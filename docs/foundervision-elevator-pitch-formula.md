# Founder Vision — Elevator Pitch Formula: Problem, Product, Result

**Source:** https://www.facebook.com/share/r/1ZnsPx68Sw/
**Transcribed:** 2026-03-22

## The Formula

**Problem -> Product -> Result**

Don't start with the product or its features. Start with the problem.

> "Anybody who's struggling with [PROBLEM] should [PRODUCT] so that they can [RESULT]."

## Example

> "Anybody who has a dog barking every time somebody knocks on their door should hire me because I can stop that dog from barking in only two sessions so that whenever somebody knocks on your door, there's peace and calm."

## Full Transcript

Here's the formula for a really great elevator pitch. Problem, product, result. So you don't start with the product, you don't start with the features of the product — you actually say: "Hey, anybody who's struggling with this should buy this so that they can experience this." Anybody who has a dog barking every time somebody knocks on their door should hire me because I can stop that dog from barking in only two sessions so that whenever somebody knocks on your door, there's peace and calm. That's an elevator pitch.
