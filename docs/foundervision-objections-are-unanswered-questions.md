# Founder Vision — Objections Are Unanswered Questions

**Source:** https://www.facebook.com/share/r/18nv513Gdj/
**Transcribed:** 2026-03-22

## The Concept

An objection is an **infected question that did not get answered** in the presentation or the close.

## The Script

1. "Based on what you've seen so far, what questions, if any, do you have?"
2. They ask a question. Answer it.
3. "What other questions do you have?" — answer that one too.
4. Repeat: "What other questions do you have?"

**The more times they give you a question and you answer it, the more likely they are to buy.** You are eliminating objections one by one.

> "Unanswered questions are like an open wound that doesn't get cleaned."
