# Founder Vision — VSL on Thank You Page (Show-Up Rate Hack)

## The Tactic

The single most valuable thing you can do when someone signs up: **put a video on the thank you page.**

Don't just list "follow me on Instagram, go to my podcast" — they've already said YES. Give them a payoff.

### The Video Script

1. "Thank you so much for doing this"
2. "You made a great decision by doing this"
3. "Here are the three things that are going to happen"
4. "When you come on this call, here are the four things you can do"

**It reaffirms the reason why they signed up.** Your call show-up rate will go through the roof if you just put a VSL right after.

## Full Transcript

The single most valuable thing that you can do when someone signs up for anything in your world — right after they sign up, on the thank you page — is to have a video. Instead of just saying "hey, here are the four things: follow me on Instagram, go to my podcast, do this, do that" — they're already in the mindset of having been pre-framed to something. Maybe they bought or registered or whatever. They said yes to something and you've got to give them a payoff. We put a video on that page that says: "Thank you so much for doing this. You made a great decision by doing this. Let me tell you what — here are the three things that are going to happen. And when you come on this call, here are the four things you can do." Now it reaffirms the reason why they did that. Your call show-up rate will go through the roof if you just put a VSL right after.
