# Founder Vision — Stories vs Carousels vs Reels Distribution

**Source:** https://www.facebook.com/share/r/1C3Y5YKtvx/
**Transcribed:** 2026-03-22

## Distribution Rules

| Format | Shown To | Gets You | Builds |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| Stories | Followers | Leads | Connection |
| Carousels | Followers, then non-followers | Engagement | Authority |
| Reels | Non-followers, then followers | Followers | Reach |

## Full Transcript

Stories get shown to followers, carousels get shown to followers and then non-followers, reels get shown to non-followers and then followers. Stories get leads, carousels get engagement, reels get followers. Stories build connection, carousels provide authority.
