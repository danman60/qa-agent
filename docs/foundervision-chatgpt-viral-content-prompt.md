## The Insight

Don't ask ChatGPT for "30 viral content ideas" — ChatGPT doesn't know what "viral" means.

Instead: ask for **50 specific, nuanced, observable moments** your audience experiences.

## The Prompt Pattern

> Give me 50 of the most observable moments [YOUR AUDIENCE] experiences when dealing with [THEIR PROBLEM]. Rank them from most common (many people relate), some (moderate), and few (niche but intense).

The key word is **observable** — real moments people recognize in their daily life, not abstract concepts.
