## The Concept

The best salespeople in the world do **seamless selling** — they sell so masterfully that you think you were born wanting it.

- You weren't born wanting an iPhone
- You weren't born wanting the brand of car you drive
- Somebody sold it to you

### What Bad Salespeople Do

- **Convincing** = what people resort to when they're bad at selling
- **High pressure** = what people resort to when they're bad at selling

### The Truth

> "People love to buy and they love to be sold. Everything you've ever bought — somebody sold it to you. They just made you think it was your idea."
