## The Formula

1. **Find** a big pool of people with a problem that's painful
2. **Obsess** over solving it so you can solve it in the most elegant way
3. **Let them know** you've created the solution
4. **Remove the risk** from them participating in that solution

> "It's virtually impossible for you not to become wealthy."
